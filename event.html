Krishna Janmashtami, also known as Gokulashtami, is an annual Hindu festival that celebrates the birth of Krishna, the eighth avatar of Vishnu.Krishna Janmashtami is followed by the festival Nandotsav, which celebrates the occasion when Nandababa distributed gifts to the community in honour of the birth.

Diwali

Dipotsav- 56 Bhog

The Dev Deepavali is the festival of Kartik Poornima celebrated in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh, India. It falls on the full moon of the Hindu month of Kartika (November - December). The main rituals performed by devotees consist of kartik snan (taking a holy bath in the Yamuna during Kartika) and deepdan (offering of oil lighted lamps) to Yamuna in the evening. The Yamuna aarti is also performed in the evening.

Mathura 30 days 30 evenings
